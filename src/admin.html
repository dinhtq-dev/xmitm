DNS Redirection

Toggle ON to redirect tool traffic through MITM (adds to /etc/hosts). Toggle OFF to restore normal routing. Independent from MITM start/stop.

Tip: DNS entries persist independently — they stay active even when MITM is stopped. On Windows, toggling DNS may show a UAC prompt — approve it or the hosts file will not change. Status reflects the real hosts file, not browser cache.
